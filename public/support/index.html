About Gymetra

Gymetra is a modern scoring system designed for gymnastics competitions, training environments, and local gyms.

The platform allows judges, coaches, and meet directors to manage scoring sessions, athlete rotations, and competition results in real time.

Common Topics

  • Connecting judges to the host device
  • Starting or activating a meet session
  • Assigning judges to events
  • Correcting scores during a competition
  • Broadcasting results live
  • Printing score sheets and reports

Community

Gymetra was built for the entire gymnastics community — athletes, judges, coaches, parents, and meet organizers.
